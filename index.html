Welcome to NeoShuyuan · Classical AI Lab

我从小在私塾学习，没有经历过传统的小学教育。每天的功课是诵读《三字经》《千字文》，用毛笔临摹甲骨文和金文。
老师教我们通过汉字的演变理解古人的思维方式，比如"日"字从太阳的象形而来，"明"由"日"和"月"组合表示光亮。

后来接触电脑和编程时，我惊讶地发现：文言文的简洁结构与编程语言有着奇妙的相似。这个发现让我开始尝试用技术来解读传统经典，特别是古代数学著作。

现在，我正在开发一系列工具：

  • 将《九章算术》的文言题目转换为现代数学表达式
  • 用可视化方式展示汉字演变与数学符号的关联
  • 为古籍中的数学问题制作交互式解题动画
这些项目不是为了取代传统学习，而是希望搭建一座桥梁，让更多人能够轻松理解这些珍贵的文化遗产。

I studied at a traditional Chinese academy without attending regular primary school. My daily lessons included reciting the *Three Character Classic* and copying oracle bone scripts.

I was amazed to discover the structure of classical Chinese is surprisingly similar to programming syntax. This inspired me to use technology to explore classical works, especially ancient mathematics.

I’m now developing tools to:

  • Convert classical problems from *The Nine Chapters* into modern math notation
  • Visualize the evolution of Chinese characters and their links to math symbols
  • Create animated step-by-step solutions for ancient math texts
These tools aim not to replace tradition, but to build bridges that make heritage more accessible.
