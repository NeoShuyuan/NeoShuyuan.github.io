Welcome to NeoShuyuan · Classical AI Lab

I studied at a traditional Chinese academy as a child. My daily lessons included reciting the Three Character Classic and copying oracle bone inscriptions.

Our teacher showed us how ancient characters like “日” (sun) evolved into concepts like “明” (bright).

When I encountered programming later, I found its structure oddly similar to Classical Chinese.

Now, I develop tools to reinterpret ancient math:

  • Transforming The Nine Chapters into modern expressions
  • Visualizing links between Chinese characters and math notation
  • Creating interactive animations for ancient problem solving
These tools are a bridge between tradition and technology.
